## Pricing Strategy Notes

- **199 Robux** is the sweet-spot entry-level — almost every Roblox kid has 200+ Robux from a starter purchase.
- **499 / 599** are the "mid whale" tier — converts well from players who hit ~5 hours of playtime.
- **999** is the ultra-whale tier. Even 0.5% conversion on this = massive revenue per DAU.
- **Show 999 first in the shop UI** (anchoring effect makes 199 look like a steal).
- Run **40% off launch sale** for first 7 days only. FOMO drives purchases.
